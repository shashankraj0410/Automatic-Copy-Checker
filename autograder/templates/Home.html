About

Smart Grading Exam Evaluation with Precision

The Automated Copy Grader System is designed to automate the process of grading students work by comparing it with the teacher's correct answers using Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology and natural language processing techniques and to facilitate the grading process of exams using Python. This application serves as a solution to enhance the efficiency and accuracy of grading, By automating the grading of multiple-choice, true/false, short answers and paragraph answers.
